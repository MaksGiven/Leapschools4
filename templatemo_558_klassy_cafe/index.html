About Gender Equality

Equality has no gender. So join the majority and vote for equality

Gender equality is first and foremost a human right. It implies that males, females and members of the LBGTQ+ community of any economic status and/or race are treated equally. This means that they have equal value and equal access to resources, freedom and opportunities. Gender inequality has become one of the most common social issues and is unfortunately the most ignored social issue. It's not only women who are affected by gender inequality - all genders are impacted, including men, trans and gender-diverse people. People may put most of their focus on other social issues like gender based violence, hunger, poverty and rape but gender inequality is what often causes these social issues. It is up to us to put a stop to gender inequality and empower people of every gender
